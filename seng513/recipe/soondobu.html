Soondobu

Article by: Kim Yu Na

DINNER

TOTAL TIME
45 MIN

6

DIFFICULTY
EASY

Soon Dobu is a hella lit dish unlike any other boring soup dish you've had before! Brought over to the United States in the 1990s by Korean immigrants in Los Angeles, the dish will bring a tear to your eyes.

This Korean stew is made with freshly curdled soft tofu as its main point, with vegetables, seafood and meats. Adding a raw egg just before serving helps provide it that extra oomph. Extra soft tofu, called soondubu in Korean, is softer than other types of tofu and is usually sold in tubes.

Prepare this Korean jjigae recipe and enjoy a taste of one of Korea's best selling comfort foods. This tasty dish is so popular, Daniel hasn't had it for several years which is pretty sad, not gonna lie :(
